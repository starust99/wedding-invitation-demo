# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Khach moi du dam cuoi cua Nhat va Phuong, gom ong ba, co chu bac, anh chi, ban be, dong nghiep va cac gia dinh duoc moi rieng bang link doc ban. Nguoi dung phu la gia dinh va co dau chu re khi quan ly danh sach khach, RSVP, luu tru va album sau tiec.

## Product Purpose

Website la thiep cuoi online cao cap, giup khach nhan loi moi rieng, xac nhan tham du, dang ky ho tro luu tru neu can, xem thong tin phu hop voi phan hoi va sau nay nhan album dung nhom khach. Thanh cong la khach cam thay duoc ton trong, hieu ro viec can lam, gui phan hoi khong bi lung tung, va gia dinh co du du lieu de lam viec voi nha hang, resort.

## Brand Personality

Tinh te, am ap, trang trong vua du. Giong mot loi moi duoc can nhac ky, co tinh gia dinh Viet Nam, khong lanh, khong suong, khong kieu form hanh chinh.

## Anti-references

Khong giong dashboard SaaS, landing page startup, mau thiep cuoi chung chung, loi moi may moc, nut bam kieu ky thuat, hoac copy qua than mat voi nguoi lon tuoi. Khong hien thong tin nhay cam nhu so ban neu khong can. Khong nhac chi phi voi khach trong ghi chu luu tru.

## Design Principles

Moi cau chu phai giu phep lich su theo vai ve: nguoi lon duoc kinh moi, ban be duoc moi gan gui, anh chi duoc moi le phep, em/chau duoc moi than tinh.

Thong tin rieng chi mo khi khach da xac nhan tham du. Truoc RSVP, trang rieng chi can gio don khach va thong tin chung.

Form RSVP phai ngan gon, ro viec, khong tao cam giac khach dang dien thu tuc hanh chinh.

Admin phai dung tieng Viet de hieu va de import: ten hien thi, cach xung ho tren thiep, nguoi dung moi, nhom xem album.

Album sau tiec phai di theo dung link va dung nhom khach, khong de khach thay minh bi xem nhu mot dong data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Uu tien tieng Viet co dau day du, cau ngan, nut bam ro hanh dong, loi loi form lich su va chi ro muc can sua. Giam phu thuoc vao animation, giu tuong phan du de doc tren mobile, va tranh cach noi co the gay ap luc cho khach khi khong the tham du.
